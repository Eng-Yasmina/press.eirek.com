<p><strong class="additional-content-header">Introducing ARChive:</strong></p>
<p class="additional-content-paragraph-1"><strong>Archive Proceedings of Academic Research Community (ARChive)</strong> is a journal published on behalf of researchers that perpetually make an effort to contribute to their fields. It serves to cultivate, propagate, and essentially archive academic research that has been authored and submitted for an academic conference. </p>
<p class="additional-content-paragraph-2">Academic conferences are a major source of cutting edge research, it is for that reason that<strong> IEREK</strong>, ARChive mother company, has made it its duty to publish proceedings given their importance as topical status reports that provide the world easy access to information that may not ever appear in traditional journals.</p>
<p class="additional-content-paragraph-3">ARChive takes pride in being a major source of readily available research that cultivates the efforts and skills of esteemed participants in International conferences. This is an opportunity to educate and be enlightened by all the different participants that do not stop at anything to make a difference in the world by their contributions to their fields.</p>
<p><strong class="additional-content-header">Benefits for your conference:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Your conference proceedings will receive maximum exposure by being published in a journal that is dedicated to impact the academic society</li>
<li>You will be published promptly</li>
<li>Free access to all users</li>
<li>Authors keep full copyright over their papers</li>
</ul>
<p><strong class="additional-content-header">Benefits of being published in ARChive:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Published papers are peer-reviewed by a selection of highly reputable professors and scholars from universities around the world</li>
<li>Free access to all users</li>
<li>Speedy publishing process: eligible papers will be published promptly and will be readily available online</li>
<li>Technical services: offering you a series of steps to help you write a highly competent research paper</li>
<li>Authors keep full copyright over papers published in ARChive</li>
</ul>
<p>Publishers are urged to have a wholesome understanding of copyrights.</p>
